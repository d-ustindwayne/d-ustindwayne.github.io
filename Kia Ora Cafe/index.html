A taste of Aotearoa

Experience the warm spirit of New Zealand at Kia Ora Cafe. Founded by Jasmine Sison Taboy, we bring authentic Kiwi culture to all 11 branches across the Philippines. Discover our nature-inspired spaces where great coffee meets meaningful connections. From charitable initiatives to community events, we're more than just a cafe - we're a place where cultures connect and communities thrive.

Genuine Kiwi Delicacies

Indulge in a unique culinary journey where New Zealand's fresh, vibrant cuisine meets Filipino comfort classics. Our menu celebrates the best of both worlds- from authentic Kiwi pies and brunch favorites to locally-inspired creations using the finest Philippine ingredients. Every dish tells a story of cultural fusion, crafted with the same warmth and care we pour into every cup of coffee.
